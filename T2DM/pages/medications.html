Understanding Your Diabetes Treatment

A guide to the different types of medicines that help manage blood sugar.

Your Plan is Unique

Your doctor will create a treatment plan just for you. It may include one or more of these medicines, along with healthy eating and physical activity. This guide is for information only—always follow your doctor's advice.

This is a simplified guide. For complete information, please visit the official page from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases (NIDDK).
